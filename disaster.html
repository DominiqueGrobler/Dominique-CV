OVERVIEW

The Disaster Alleviation website has been designed to keep records of different donations being made to Disasters listed, show current active disasters and allow allocation of resources to disasters. Below is some of the pages that can be found on the foundations website. Donations can be made by individuals, companies, other organisations, and even governments. Donations can be goods (clothes, non‐perishable foods, etc.) or monetary. For instance, goods could be sent to assist in a disaster and \ money can be spent (for instance, to a neighborhood nonprofit) or used to buy things that are subsequently delivered.
